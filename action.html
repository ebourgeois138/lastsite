Action

All three of my kids were multi-sport athletes. This means I have seen hundreds of lacrosse, soccer, & basketball games, and spent hours at the track. Experiencing all of these games has given me a perspective into the sports world unlike any other. The comradery, teamwork, and passion that can be captured in one photo is amazing. I didn't fully dive into my photography hobby until my youngest daughter was in highschool, so you will see a lot of her team photos throughout this page.
