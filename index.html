Hi, I'm Betsy

I have been photographing memories for years.

In recent years, my fascination with photography has only grown. I've taken classes, gone through various cameras, and even followed around a National Geographic photographer for a day! At first I took photos to preserve the most beautiful moments with my family. I still enjoy doing that, but I have taken it a step further.

Please look through my site and admire the photos I have captured over the years. From hundreds of sports games around Massachusetts, to the warm waters of Bermuda, I have been everywhere and seen everything. I take pride in my photos and hope you can enjoy them as much as I do.
